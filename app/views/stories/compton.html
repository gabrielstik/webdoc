Compton is a city in southern Los Angeles County, California, United States. It’s one of the oldest cities in the county of LA. Its population is currently in majority composed of afro-american young people (and since ‘50 declaration prohibiting racial exclusion), people decided to take their chance in middle-class suburbs to improve their way of life.

1959
From 1959 and the first black mayor in Compton (predominantly inhabited by white families from middle class before) and until then, Compton became a truly black culture’ nevralgic point for the West Coast of the U.S. Leading to the development of rap music and hip-hop culture.

Its signs a turnover of the promising youth that grew up in the ‘80s and ‘90s in thoses suburbs. Street
poetry
Compton is the cradle of Gangsta Rap and G-funk, characterized by texts about violence talking about gangs’ war. Many rap artists' careers started in Compton, including N.W.A (Eazy-E, MC Ren, Dr. Dre, Ice Cube, DJ Yella), Coolio, DJ Quik, Nishant, Jeeves, The Game, Kendrick Lamar, YG, and Compton's Most Wanted. It’s a crucial point in the history of the West Coast Legacy.
